Beacons In Bounds

We ski and snowboard at resorts when the snow falls expecting that because the rope has dropped and patrol have opened a slope that it's safe. Except it's not always and we believe that wearing a beacon can help with the worst happens.

A beacon can't stop an avalanche but it can help with precious minutes during a rescue situation. It's really not that hard just throw your beacon on when you getting your boots on, leave it running when you are out shredding.

The ways to help

Everyone

When you ski on a powder day at a resort please wear a beacon, talk to your friends and family about the advantages of wearing a beacon and know how to use it.

Post to social media and group chat's #beaconsinbounds, get the word out and keep the conversation going.

Make this your own, we started this and really don't care about owning it we just want people to stay safe and keep you safe.

Those with a story to tell

If you have had a good or bad experience and would like to share

Patrollers, Educators and others with avalanche knowledge

If you have the knowledge to help with this website and think we can do better in any aspect please send us a message and we will do our best to update the message we send out.

Resorts, brands and Organizations

If you wish to and have the ability to help financially all we are asking for is printed materials, stickers, signage, apparel please get in touch
